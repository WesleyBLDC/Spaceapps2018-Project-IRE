Are you Wildfire ready?
  • Sign up on our website to get notice on nearby wildfires
  • Know your community’s evacuation plans and shelter locations.
  • Gather emergency supplies, including N95 respirator masks and medication. Don’t forget the needs of pets.
  • Designate a room that can be closed off from outside air to keep indoor pollution levels low.
  • Keep important documents in a fireproof, safe place. Create password-protected digital copies.
  • Use fire-resistant materials to build, renovate, or make repairs.
  • Create a fire-resistant zone that's free of leaves, debris, or flammable materials for at least 30 feet from your home.
  • Review insurance coverage to make sure it's enough to replace your property.
